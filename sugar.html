Sugar Factory 2.0

CONCEPT

Making the world a sweeter place. A new concept for a candy-themed American Brasserie in New York City and Las Vegas where customers create their own experience at the Sugar Factory.

HOW DID IT START?

Sugar Factory serves fantastical sugary milkshakes, mixed drinks, desserts as well as comfort food that brings you back to your childhood.

MAKE YOUR OWN EXPERIENCE

The new identity aims to open up creativity, allowing customers to make their own memorable experience at the Sugar Factory.

VISUALS

Bringing the brand to life with vibrant colors and playful patterns transporting you into the endless world of imagination.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Sugar Factory is expanding its target audience as we believe candies and sweets are not limited to age. The new innovative f&b concept welcomes all ages with the simplistic yet playful visuals.

A TOUCH OF GLAMOR

Candies are associated to happiness, comfort and friendship. Today, taking a break from all the stress and worry in our lives is not a luxury that we can all afford. To highlight the symbolism of sweets, a touch of gold is added to the visuals and packaging as well as to bring out the WOW factor

INTERIOR

We believe that once you step into the Sugar Factory, you transport back to your favorite childhood memories while also bringing you to the future with its innovative food and beverage.
