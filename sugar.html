Sugar Factory 2.0

PROJECT DETAILS

Making the world a sweeter place. A new concept for a candy-themed American Brasserie that serves fantastical milkshakes, sugary drinks, desserts and comfort food in New York City and Las Vegas where customers create their own experience with friends and family at the Sugar Factory.

Reinventing
Sugar Factory is an iconic restaurant where many friends and families go for celebration, and celebrities go for a sugar rush. The rebrand highlights the experience customers have with excitable, innovative and unique treats.

Make It Your Own Experience
The new identity aims to open up creativity, allowing customers to make their own memorable experience at the Sugar Factory.

Bringing The Brand To Life
Sweets are not limited by age. The new innovative F&B concept welcomes all ages. The vibrant and playful visuals transports customers into an endless world of imagination.

Candies are associated to happiness, comfort and friendship. To highlight that, a touch of gold is added to the visuals and packaging as well as to bring out the WOW factor.

Interior
Keeping the interior design clean and simple to highlight the food and beverage.
