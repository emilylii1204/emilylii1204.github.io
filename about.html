about shop design
Hello! I'm Emily and I'm a Graphic Designer and Illustrator based in Hong Kong/New York City.
I started off drawing with pen and paper to brighten peoples' day. Then I discovered how powerful a simple drawing or design can be: as an inspiration, as a motivator for people to do something greater. So I practiced, I experimented and I drew and designed some more. I bring in both analog and digital techniques together in most of my work. Together, it is a tactile approach to illustrating the narrative and to connect people to people and people to product
