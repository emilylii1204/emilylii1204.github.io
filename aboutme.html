Hello! I'm a
multidisciplinary designer
I'm from Hong Kong and currently living in New York. I was drawn to the field of design due to a personal experience: Remember those dense textbooks we used to have in class? I was never able to retain information as quickly as my friends. This challenge sparked my interest in finding alternative and more engaging ways to communicate and absorb information. Design provided a means to visually convey complex ideas, making concepts more accessible and memorable. This journey into design was fueled by a desire to create materials that are not only informative but also captivating, allowing others to grasp and remember content more effectively than traditional methods allowed.
