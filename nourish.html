Nourish Me

PROJECT DETAILS

Nourish me is tablet game that teaches health and nutrition to all ages.

Industry
Education
Health

UI/UX Design
Illustration

Why A Game?
You never stop learning.

VISUALS

The app is filled with lively, vibrant and playful characters that helps engage young ones while feeding them with knowledge. Much more fun than a textbook right?

WIREFRAME

The platform must be simple enough that a 4 year old can click through from start to finish.

a hand-drawn typeface instead of digital for a more approachable design

LEARN IT FIRST THEN PLAY

The app has two parts: learning and playing the game. Click on “I want to learn” to learn about each macronutrient and in detail of each produce.

THE GAME

The game has multiple levels, each level has 5-10 questions. Every time you complete a level, you are rewarded with a produce and/or points to purchase a produce from the market or unlock an avatar!

WHAT DO YOU GET OUT OF IT?

Once you collect enough produce, you can unlock nutrient-densed recipes that you can easily make at home with your friends and families!
