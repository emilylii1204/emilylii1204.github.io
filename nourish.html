Nourish Me

Nourish me is an educational game on health and nutrition for ages from 7 and above. With so many sources and paragraphs after paragraphs of information on the topic, it can be a dull subject to read and learn about. This interactive game is fun and educational at the same time where you unlock avatars, collect produce and recipes.

Nourish Me teaches in a form of a game and is divided into two parts: learning component and game component.

Bringing the knowledge to real life: once collected enough produce, you can unlock delicious and nutritiously fileld recipies to make at home.
