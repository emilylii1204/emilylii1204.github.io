Nourish Me

PROJECT DETAILS

Nourish me is tablet game that teaches health and nutrition to all ages.

Industry
Education
Health
Category
UI/UX Design
Illustration

Interactive Learning
There are too many sources about health and nutrition with paragraphs after paragraphs of information. Who would want to read that? Nourish Me teaches as a form of a game and is divided into two parts: learning and playing the game.

Going Into The Real Life
Once collected enough produce, you can unlock healthy. recipies you can make at home.
