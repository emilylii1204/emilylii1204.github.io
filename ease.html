ease

CONCEPT

ease provides feminine hygiene products that encourages all girls and women to be confident with their bodies and to live their lives without the monthly fuss. To ease it into your daily lives.

HOW DID IT START?

Women were taught to hide their periods or to feel ashamed of having them. But why? Why is it such a big deal? ease believes a woman should be proud of being who they are and of their bodies and should not be brought down any times, especially during the time of the month.

BRAND OVERVIEW

ease promotes confidence and empowerment to all girls and women. The bold and vibrant colors, the elegant choice of typeface and the simplicity of the products helps integrate their monthly cycle into their lives at ease.

VISUALS

Colors and shapes affect emotions. Steering away from the stereotypical feminine pink, the soft pastel sandpink along with a calming blue and an empowering orange red lifts spirits up while also relaxes the user. The pattern is associated to the intensity of the flow.

the liners and pads are color coded on the backing paper and bags for easy identification, indicating which you should use dpeending on the intensity of the flow.

PACKAGING

pad packaging is made with recycled plastic and can easily dispose used pads hygienically by tightening the strings on both ends into a little pouch

ease integrates feminine hygiene into your daily lives as if it is just another day. The pad and tampon boxes are simple solid colors.

PRODUCTS

products that help integrate your monthly cycle and making your it easy for you without all the fuss.

DIGITAL

Let us guide you and take care of you. wW can help determine the product and quantity based on your needs. All you need to do is fill out a simple questionnaire on the website or mobile app. The app can also help you track your monthly cycle.
