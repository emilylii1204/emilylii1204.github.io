Care-ot Tips

Why are we so busy all the time?

CONCEPT

Work take a toll on our mental health.
Care-ot Tips is a mobile app that helps you get through mental struggles with the help of a friendly, whitty and insightful character.

CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT

Someone who you can trust and provide a sense of comfort.

HOW IT WORKS

Point and scan your surroundings to look for Carrot Tops.
He will remind you to love yourself and elevate your worries.

You can also introduce Carrot Tops to your friends and families.
