Care-ot Tips

CONCEPT

Life is getting busier and stressful. But most of the time we forget to take care of ourselves. Care-ot Tips is a mobile app that helps you get through mental challenges and struggles by giving you daily self-care tips.

HOW DID IT START?

As children, we hug our fluffy toys for comfort and we see them as companions. Even on occasions when we get lonely, we create an imaginary friend, someone we can count on when no one else can. Care-ot Tips is built upon the idea of a companion.

CHARACTER DESIGN

Carrot Tops cares about you and your well being. He is there to make you laugh when youâ€™re down: he is there to share happy moments with you.
Carrot Tops is designed as a whitty, empathetic, optimistic character. Incorporating what makes a character cute and approachable: round and smooth lines, beady eyes, humanizing an animal and an unexpected feature (carrot as a tail).

HOW IT WORKS

Just point and scan your surroundings. Wherever you are, you will find Carrot Tops and he will give you a care tip on how to love and respect yourself, take care of mind and body or crack a joke to brighten your day.

The app configures your surrounding to make Carrot tops more realistic and adapt into the surrounding.

You can also introduce Carrot Tops to your friends and families.
