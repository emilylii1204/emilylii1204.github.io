Care-ot Tips

PROJECT DETAILS

Care-ot Tips is a mobile app that helps people get through daily or occasional mental struggles with the help of a whitty and insightful character. Whereever you are: at home, school, work or on business trips, Carrot Tops will be there.

Say Hello To Mr. Carrot Tops
He is someone who you can trust, to provide a sense of comfort to get you through the day and make you smile whenever you see him.

Self Care At Your Fingertips
Point your phone and scan around your surroundings to look for Carrot Tops. He will remind you to love yourself and make your day a little better.

The More The Merrier
