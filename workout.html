Pulse motion: Fitness watch interface
UI design
Icon
Responsive design
Statistic design
Fitness and wellness
The inspiration for this concept came from the limitations in functionality of the Apple Watch's fitness interface. The goal is to create an innovative and user-friendly platform that makes tracking workouts more enjoyable and engaging.The proposed feature combines the beauty of motion with advanced fitness tracking capabilities. It offers a unique and immersive way to enhance the workout experience. Imagine experiencing a new dimension of exercise where your movements generate mesmerizing abstract patterns on your screen. With this feature, every workout becomes a captivating masterpiece, turning fitness into a creative and enjoyable activity right in the palm of your hand.
