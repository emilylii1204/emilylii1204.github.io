Sugar Factory 2.0

Making the world a sweeter place. Sugar factory is candy-themed American Brasserie that serves fantastical milkshakes, sugary drinks, desserts and comfort food in New York City and Las Vegas. The rebrand highlights the innovative food and experience that customers have and share with their friends and family
