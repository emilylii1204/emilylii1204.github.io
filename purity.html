Purity Water

CONCEPT

The graceful flow of the water; the elegant swish of a brush. Purity highlights the beauty of water, promoting from nature to bottle.

HOW IT ALL STARTED

Water is a necessity. We drink water to hydrate, to energize, to get through our days. We believe that there is a beauty to everything. Water is an element to energy, it is one of the five elements in Chinese Philosophy and Culture. It represents agility, strength and wisdom. It creates life, it sustains life.

LOGO

Experimenting with the movement of the brush, the pressure, the water to ink ratio to imitate the feeling water evokes and its movement.

A loose wrist and fast motion creates an elegant tone. Using pure acrylic paint creates a textural brushstroke that imitates the one direction movement of water.

VISUALS

complimenting with the elegant type are simple thin ink illustrations of plants.

EMBRACING NATURE (OF THE MATERIAL)

The reflection and shadow of the bottle imitates flowing water while casting the reflection of the plants illustration to mimick the reflection of plants and flowers on the surface of a pond and lake.
