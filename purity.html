Purity Water

There is a beauty in filtering water that flows through underground pipes, the journey it takes from miles away. Purity is a filling filtered water service that sees recycling a magnificent process. The elegant swish of the paint brush imitates the flow of water.

In Chinese Philosophy, water is the second element amongst the five elements that makes up everything in nature. One stroke expresses three things water stands for: agility, strength and wisdom.

Water creates life and sustains it. Water grows plants bringing them to life. The illustrations of flowers represent water's ability to grow plants, creating and sustaining life.
